VED

VED appears to have come from the Aztec C65 C compiler distribution and to have been modified by several people along the way, including porting to FLEX and making it more like vi. The source code was distributed (as VEDSRC.ARC) in the C65 distribution and is, therefore, assumed to be open source. According to the Wikipedia "Aztec C remains copyrighted and has not been placed into the public domain. Harry Suckow, who started Manx Software Systems with partners Thomas Fenwick and James Goodnow II, is the copyright holder."
